Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Begin2Code is an educational platform created to help beginners learn programming and computer science concepts. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using Begin2Code, you agree to the practices described below.

We may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and login details when you create an account. We also collect general usage information like pages visited and interactions with learning resources to improve the learning experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide and improve our educational services. This includes managing your account, giving access to notes and learning materials, tracking progress, and communicating important updates related to Begin2Code.

4. Data Sharing

Begin2Code does not sell or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only with trusted services required for hosting, analytics, or security purposes, or when legally required.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. While no online platform can guarantee complete security, we continuously work to maintain a safe and reliable environment for learners.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You may also choose to stop using the platform at any time.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Begin2Code grows. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the platform means you accept the updated policy.
